Welcome to the Castle Forbes Bay Recreation Club website

Affectionately known as `The Rec Club` is a place where the local community gathers and has a great ol' time…

We host such things as movie nights, Friday Games Night, an annual Art & Music Exhibition, annual garage sale, regular archery, cider presses, cider tastings & annual cider makers challenge and many more. See our events page for further information.

But… you could also host your own private or public event. Get in touch if you have an idea.
